# Sientific Photo Classificator

I need an app called **FieldTaxa** that allows me to:

- Take Photo's or video's or 
- Reuse Photo's video's available in the camera roll
- In a future version it shall be possible to import Photo's/videos managed/stored in the SpringEvalApp

It shall then be possible to "Classify" those photo's by applying categories that shall be configurable. As an example it shall be possible to create a category tree similar to this one:

- Animals, Insects, terrestrial
- Animals, Insects, acquatic, Class, Order, Familly, genus
- Animals, bird, seed feeding
- Animals, bird, rapaces
- Plants, tree
- Plants, mountain flowers
- Springs, Holocrene
- Springs, Rheocrenes
- : : :

Functionality:

-  Each photo video shall/can be tagged according to the above category tree
-  it shall then be possible to search for any of the category tree entry (or entry combination).
-  It shall be possible to see the "Search history (with date)" as well
-  View the photos/videos from within the app
- There shall be some configuaration possibilities that allow the user to:

  -  Work with photo/video stopred in the app only (in such a case it shall be possible to "import" existing photo/video from the camera roll)
  -  Work with photo/video stored in the camera roll (So the app will have to reference the photo/video that will be phisically stored in the camera roll. In such a case if the photo/video is deleted, the corresponding entry in the app shall be deleted to avoid orphan items)
  -  Work with bot in parallel (Photo's/Video's in bot the app and camera roll)
  -  Configure/set the category tree (it could be done in the app directly without the need to goi through a specific settings menu entry)
  -  The following settings shall be available: Light/Dark mode either automatic (system) or manually settable
  -  The App shall support multiple languages:
     -  DE, IT, FR, EN Shall be available by default
     -  Language is set to the device language by default (English if the correct language is not available)
     -  But it shall be possible to change the App langiage manually
  -  The App shall be available for Apple and Android. 
  -  App Technology shall be flutter
